# AIR 16-Question Core Questionnaire

> Selected from the 60-question full version, 4 per dimension (4 different facets), 8 Forward + 8 Reverse
>
> Date: 2026-03-25

---

## Selection Criteria

1. **Maximize facet coverage**: 4 of 5 facets per dimension for content validity
2. **Perfect direction balance**: 2F+2R per dimension, 8F/8R total = 50/50
3. **High discrimination priority**: Concrete behavioral descriptions, clear option gradients, universally applicable
4. **Avoid cross-dimension contamination**: Exclude questions that might measure two dimensions
5. **Group by dimension**: 4 questions per dimension presented consecutively, D1→D2→D3→D4 order, easier first within each

---

## Legend

- **D1** = Learnability (E/T)
- **D2** = Evaluation Objectivity (O/S)
- **D3** = Risk Tolerance (F/R)
- **D4** = Human Presence (P/H)
- **Forward**: Option 1 = least AI-replaceable → Option 5 = most AI-replaceable (raw score used)
- **Reverse**: Option 1 = most AI-replaceable → Option 5 = least AI-replaceable (scored as 6 - raw)
